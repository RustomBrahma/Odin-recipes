Chicken Friedrice

Description

Chicken Fried Rice is a popular Asian dish made with stir-fried rice, chicken, vegetables, and a savory sauce. This dish is quick to prepare and perfect for using up leftover rice. The combination of tender chicken, crisp vegetables, and flavorful rice makes it a satisfying meal for any time of the day.

This recipe will guide you through the steps to make delicious Chicken Fried Rice at home. It's a versatile dish that you can customize with your favorite vegetables and seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 cup peas
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken breast and cook until fully cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the chopped onion, bell pepper, and carrots. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the minced garlic and peas to the pan. Stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes until the garlic is fragrant.
  4. Push the vegetables to the side of the pan and pour the beaten eggs into the empty space. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked, then mix them with the vegetables.
  5. Add the cooked rice and chicken back to the pan. Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Stir everything together until well combined and heated through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
